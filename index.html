Hi! I am Eungyu Park

Summary

I'm a Master of Science in Information (MSI) student at the University of Michigan - Ann Arbor specializing in User-Centered Agile Development and User Experience (UX) Design. My goal at the University of Michigan is to learn how users can easily interact with technologies, study emerging technologies like AR/VR, and apply my knowledge into a real business through several projects.


My passion is in Uer Experience (UX) design, web development, and digital marketing. I have been working for Thomas Park MD & Associates since April 2019 to create, design, maintain, and update a static website as well as advertise it on Google search.


Before working for Thomas Park MD & Associates, I worked at Global Team Blue (GTB) as a Junior Implementation Analyst for one and a half years. My responsibilities were to 1. develop a metric tagging plan & implement it on Adobe Dynamic Tag Management (DTM), 2. implement Third-Party tags and JavaScript like DoubleClick and Facebook Pixel on DTM, 3. conduct Quality Assurance by using Charles and web developer tools, 4. check whether the variables fired correctly on Adobe Analytics & Adobe Mobile Analytics, and 5. create, enable, and check In-app messages on Adobe Mobile Analytics.
