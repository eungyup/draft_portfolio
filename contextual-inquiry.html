Project - Contextual Inquiry

Welcome to my Contextual Inquiry Project!

At the Contextual Inquiry class at the University of Michigan, I worked for the University of Michigan College of LSA, Undergraduate Education Division. I (1) conducted interviews with key stakeholders for the College of LSA & Henry Ford College (HFC) Transfer Bridge Program website, (2) collected and analyzed qualitative data based on the interviews with stakeholders, (3) built an affinity wall to visualize the qualitative data, and (4) reported key findings and recommendation


Below are some works from the contextual inquiry project.

Interview

My team conducted seven interviews with three different types of stakeholders: students, the LSA team, and the Henry Ford College (HFC) team.

Students:

  • (1) Prospective Transfer Student
  • (2) Current Students

LSA team:

  • (1) LSA Team Program Coordinator
  • (1) LSA Team Student Recruiter

HFC team:

  • (1) HFC Transfer Program Coordinator
  • (1) HFC Dean of Liberal arts

Affinity Wall

My team cataloged the data further by writing down each data point from the post annotation sessions on sticky notes and then grouped similar sticky notes together to visualize a pattern. After that, we constructed the affinity wall by adding additional layers of thematic overlaps between the different clusters. All the clusters followed a theme, and they were further grouped together to form the major findings.

Final Presentation

At the end of the semester, we presented our findings and recommendations to the client (College of LSA team and FHC team) and the class. We got a great compliment from the client, and they were excited to report our report to the higher executive and redesign the web site.
